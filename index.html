Freshwater Heatwave Classification Framework

Heatwave Severity Classifier

A multi-parameter biogeochemical severity scoring tool derived from long-term monitoring data of the South Basin, Lake Windermere. Calibrated per lake using correlation shifts between normal and heatwave conditions.
